# Vendor Contract: Tata Steel

**Contract ID:** VND-2024-001
**Vendor:** Tata Steel
**Material:** Steel Coil
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Expiration Date:** December 31, 2025

## 1. SLA Terms

### Delivery Performance
On-time delivery rate must be minimum 95%. Lead time is 7 business days for standard orders, 3 business days for rush orders. Orders must arrive within plus or minus 1 day of scheduled delivery to qualify as on-time.

### Quality Standards
Maximum 2% defective units per shipment. Steel coil must meet IS 2062 Grade A standards. Certificate of Conformity and Material Test Reports required with each shipment.

## 2. Penalty Structure

### Late Delivery Penalties
1-2 days late: 2% of order value. 3-5 days late: 5% of order value. 6-10 days late: 10% of order value. More than 10 days late: 15% of order value plus right to cancel.

### Quality Defect Penalties
Defect rate 2-5%: 5% credit on affected shipment. Defect rate 5-10%: 15% credit plus root cause analysis required. Defect rate above 10%: full replacement at vendor cost plus 10% penalty.

## 3. Delivery Commitments

Primary supply route originates from Jamshedpur Steel Plant in Jharkhand to Mumbai Plant (factory_1) via rail and road multimodal transport. Transit time is 5-7 business days. Backup route goes through Kolkata and activates automatically when primary route transit exceeds 10 days.

## 4. Force Majeure

Events considered force majeure with no penalties applied: natural disasters including cyclones, earthquakes, and floods. Government-imposed restrictions or lockdowns. Labor strikes at vendor facilities with maximum 15 days coverage. Vendor must notify within 24 hours of force majeure event.
